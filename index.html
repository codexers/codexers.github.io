Our Process.

Plan is on taking a max-input of 50 students and teach them a technology from beginner(noob) level to a level of decent expertise(not noob) in that field.We go in a project-based approach that benefits the students.

Our Approach.

Well-built and perfectly structured project imbibing the technology we chose in various levels will be demonstrated.Then the project will be deconstructed into atomic parts and will be explained.This way understanding the concepts will be easy and fun.
